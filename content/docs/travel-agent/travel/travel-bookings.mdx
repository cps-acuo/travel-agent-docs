## Navigating to the Travel Bookings Page

The Travel Bookings page provides an overview of all bookings made for travel packages. Here you can manage existing bookings and create new ones.
</Step>
<Step>
## Creating a new Travel Booking

To create a new booking, you typically start from a Travel Package and select the travelers. Click on the **New Booking** button within a travel package to initiate the process.

### Booking Information

When creating a booking, you will need to specify:
- **Travel Package**: The package for which the booking is being made.
- **Travelers**: The travelers who will be part of the booking.
- **Rooming**: Assign travelers to rooms.
- **Payments**: Manage the payment schedule and status for the booking.
</Step>
<Step>
## Editing a Travel Booking and Viewing Related Information

To view or edit a booking, click on it from the bookings list. This will open the booking details page, where you can update information across various tabs.

The booking detail view is organized into several sections:
- **General**: Main details of the booking, including dates and status.
- **Travelers**: List of travelers included in the booking.
- **Financials**: Detailed breakdown of costs, payments, and outstanding balances.
- **Journal**: Log of communications and notes related to the booking.
- **Actions**: Any specific actions or activities associated with the booking.
</Step>
