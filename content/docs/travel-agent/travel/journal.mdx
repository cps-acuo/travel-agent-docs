<Step>
## Understanding the Journal

The Journal is a comprehensive log of activities, communications, and notes related to various records in the system, such as travelers, bookings, and travel packages. It helps in keeping track of all interactions and changes.
</Step>
<Step>
## Creating a new Journal Entry

You can add a journal entry from the **Journal** tab of any record (e.g., a traveler or a booking). Click on **New** to create a new entry.

### Journal Entry Information

A journal entry typically includes:
- **Type**: The type of journal entry (e.g., Note, Email, Phone Call).
- **Subject**: A brief summary of the entry.
- **Message**: The detailed content of the journal entry.
- **Follow-up**: A date for any required follow-up action.
</Step>
<Step>
## Viewing and Managing Journal Entries

All journal entries are listed chronologically within the Journal tab of a record. You can view the details of each entry, edit existing entries, or delete them if necessary. This provides a complete history of all activities related to the record.
</Step>
