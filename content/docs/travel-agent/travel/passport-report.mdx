## Purpose of Passport Reports

Passport reports provide information on travel document information for all travelers in your package. 
These reports help ensure all travelers have valid passports and appropriate visas for their destinations, reducing the risk of travel disruptions.
</Step>
<Step>
## Generating a Passport Report
1. Navigate to the Passport Report section
2. Select the travel package you want to report on
3. Click **View** to compile the passport information

</Step>
