## Accessing Hotel Reports

Hotel reports provide a comprehensive overview of all accommodation bookings across your travel packages. These reports help travel agents track room allocations, special requests, and ensure all travelers have confirmed accommodations.

To access hotel reports, navigate to the Reports section in the main menu and select **Hotel Reports**.
</Step>
<Step>
## Generating a Hotel Report

To generate a new hotel report:

1. Navigate to the Hotel Report section
2. Set the filter parameters (date range, destinations, hotel properties, etc.)
3. Click on **Generate Report** to create a new report based on your criteria

The system will compile all relevant accommodation information matching your criteria into a detailed report.
</Step>
<Step>
## Working with Hotel Data

Once your hotel report is generated, you can:

- View room allocations and occupancy details
- Track special requests for each booking
- Identify any overbookings or scheduling conflicts
- Export the report in various formats (PDF, Excel, CSV) for sharing with hotels or tour operators
- Make bulk updates to room types, check-in/check-out times, or special requests
</Step>
