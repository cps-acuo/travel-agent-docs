## Purpose of the Hotel Reports

Hotel reports provide a report that can be sent to a Hotel accelerate check-in process for the hotel.

To access hotel reports, navigate to the Reports section in the main menu and select **Hotel Reports**.
</Step>
<Step>
## Generating a Hotel Report

To generate a new hotel report:

1. Navigate to the Hotel Report section
2. Select the hotel within the package.
3. Click on **View Report** to create a report based on your criteria
</Step>
