## Navigating to Payment Claims

To access all Payment Claims, click on the **Payment Claims** link in the sidebar. This section allows you to manage all claims.

</Step>
<Step>
## Creating a New Payment Claim

To create a new payment claim, click on the **New** button in the command bar. The required fields for creating a claim are:
- **Travel Package**: The travel package associated with the claim.
- **Package Order**: The order number for the travel package.

Which are found in the top left corner of the claim form, next to the owner field.



The system will generate a unique claim reference number for tracking purposes.
</Step>
<Step>
## Managing the Claims Process

Once claims are created, you can:

- Track the status of each claim through its lifecycle
- Send automated reminders for outstanding claims
- Document communications related to the claim
- Record partial payments against the claim
- Generate reports on outstanding and resolved claims
- Close claims once they are fully processed
</Step>
