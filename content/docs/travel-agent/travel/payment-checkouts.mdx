## Understanding Payment Checkouts

Payment checkouts are the final step in the booking process where travelers complete their payment for travel packages. This module helps travel agents process payments securely and efficiently, whether they're handling deposits, full payments, or installment plans.
</Step>
<Step>
## Processing a Payment Checkout

To process a payment checkout:

1. Navigate to the booking you want to process a payment for
2. Click on the **Process Payment** button
3. Select the payment method (credit card, bank transfer, cash, etc.)
4. Enter the payment amount
5. Complete any payment gateway requirements
6. Confirm the transaction

The system will process the payment and update the booking's payment status accordingly.
</Step>
<Step>
## Managing Payment Records

After processing payments, you can:

- Generate receipts and invoices for customers
- View payment history for any booking
- Process refunds when necessary
- Reconcile payments with your accounting system
- Set up automated payment reminders for upcoming installments
- Generate financial reports for completed transactions
</Step>
